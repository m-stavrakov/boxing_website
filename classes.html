Classes Schedule

Here in surbiton boxing club all boxers aged 10 and above are welcomed. We have separated our classes based on ability & age groups. In order to produce a high standard of competitive boxers, amateur boxing classes and recreational keep-fit classes are kept separate.

New joiners should visit the club to register interest before starting any training at surbiton boxing club
