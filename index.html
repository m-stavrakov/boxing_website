About Us

Welcome to Surbiton Boxing Club, your local community-based amateur sports club dedicated to promoting fitness and well-being through the art of boxing. Established in 2010, Surbiton Boxing Club proudly holds affiliation with the esteemed England Boxing organisation. Our primary mission is to foster a secure and nurturing atmosphere for the development and coaching of Olympic-style boxing techniques.

Training with us

Here in Surbiton Boxing Club we aim to build champions who can compete professionally. We are here every day and there there are classes that range from novice to professionals. In addition to building champions we also made a special heat classfor people who want to keep fit and challenge their physical abilities.
