About Us

Welcome to Surbiton Boxing Club, your local community-based amateur sports club dedicated to promoting fitness and well-being through the art of boxing. Established in 2010, Surbiton Boxing Club proudly holds affiliation with the esteemed England Boxing organization. Our primary mission is to foster a secure and nurturing atmosphere for the development and coaching of Olympic-style boxing techniques.

Training with us

At the heart of our training program is a dedicated and tightly-knit team of highly qualified coaches. Each session is carefully guided and enriched with their invaluable advice and expertise. Our coaching team is bound together by a common passion for imparting knowledge and instilling a love for the sport. Under the guidance of our Head Coach and the coaching team collaborate to shape a comprehensive boxing training program that emphasizes fundamental excellence.
